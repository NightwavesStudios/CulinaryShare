About

CulinaryShare is a web application developed for the sole purpose of making cooking more accesible. Here on CulinaryShare, we have made shure to add a wide variety of unique recipes that you can cook right in your kitchen! Furthermore, we have a maintained lessons page where you can go to learn new skills and techniques in your cooking journey!


CulinaryShare was developed by Nightwaves Studios, a programmer, and the Atlanta Droids (57000), a robotics FLL team in Atlanta, Georgia.



spa

CulinaryShare is an application that was developed for a Robotics competition's innovation portion. This website got us all the way to the Georgia FLL States for the 2023-2024 season! This applications purpose is to assist people with lessons on how to cook, as well a provide unique recipes. We developed this application to be very easy to use with begginer cooks. Unlike other sites, our site doesn't just tell you how to make the recipe, it provides you with different links to learn more about the techqniques you need to use to make the recipe!

CulinaryShare was developed by Chris Henry.
