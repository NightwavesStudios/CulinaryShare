What is Culinary Share?


Culinary Share is a website made to share lessons on how to cook, as well as a platform offering people with easy, diverse dishes that they can learn to cook right in their home! CulinaryShare was developed by the CulinaryShare Team, founded by Nightwaves Studios and the Atlanta Droids (57000).

Why Learn to Cook?



Cooking for many is more than just putting food on a plate. For some, its a hobby. Hobbies are proven to destress people, and declutter there minds. Furthermore, next time your sitting around bored, you can cook!!!

Cooking at home can save money in many ways. Firstly, cooking at home substitutes going out to restaruants, wich are way expensiver than your grocery bill. Also, cooking at home makes you aware of the ingredients, enabling you to eliminate unecessary ingredients, or buy cheaper products, also cutting down on your bill.

I almost garentee you that during some point in your life, you will find the usefulness behind cooking. Maybe its the fact that your sick of having Instant Roman in your college dorm, or that you no longer have the money to go out to dinner, but still want restaurant quality meals. Whatever the case, cooking can prove to be a good and useful life skill to learn, and its also one that isnt very time consuming to learn, and is very flexible.
