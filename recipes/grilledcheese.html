Grilled Cheese


Grilled cheese is the combination of bread and melted cheese, the perfect blend. Grilled Cheese was invented during the United State’s depression because of it’s affordability and ability to be made easily and quickly. Today, Grilled Cheese is know for its great taste with minimal effort.



Servings: 1

Cook Time: 7

Prep Time: 5

Ingredients:



Ingredients:

  • Butter or Mayo (Optional)
  • 2 slices of Bread
  • Desired Amount of Cheese


Appliances:

  • Pan
  • Stovetop




Tip: Thin Slices of Cheese or Shredded Cheese Melts Quicker



Prep Instructions:

  1. Butter the bread or put mayo on the bread
  2. Put the cheese on the inside of the 2 slices of bread
  3. Put your pan on your stovetop and turn the stove on to around medium heat.
  4. If you want, add extra butter to the pan.


Cook Instructions:

  1. Put your sandwich inside your pan
  2. Toast on the stove until the cheese is gooey and the bread is a golden brown.
  3. When the bottom is golden brown, flip the grilled cheese to cook the other side. When that side is golden brown, continue to step 4.
  4. Take off of the stove and slice if prefered
  5. Share & Enjoy!
